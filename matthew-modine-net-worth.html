Matthew Modine Net Worth

Matthew Modine is an American actor born on March 22, 1959 in Loma Linda, CA. He is best known for his roles in Weeds, Stranger Things, Birdy, and Full Metal Jacket. He gained fame for his role as Sullivan Groff on Showtime's Weeds and as Dr. Brenner in Netflix's Stranger Things. He is also known for his roles as a disturbed Vietnam vet in Birdy and Private Joker in Full Metal Jacket.
