LGM OTT Release Date, Collection, OTT Platform, Hit or Flop

Spread the love             

The world of cinema has seen a shift with movies releasing on OTT platforms, and the Tamil movie “LGM” (Let’s Get Married) is no exception. We’re here to give you all the scoop on when you can catch this film on OTT, how it performed at the box office, and whether it’s been a hit or a flop. Plus, we’ll spill the beans on which OTT platform will host “LGM” for your streaming pleasure.

LGM OTT Release Date

“LGM” stars Harish Kalyan, Ivana, and Nadhiya in a charming Tamil romantic comedy. It initially hit theaters on July 28, 2023, and fans have been eagerly awaiting its OTT release. Typically, movies land on OTT platforms about a month after their theatrical debut. So, brace yourselves because “Let’s Get Married” is expected to pop up on an OTT platform around late August or early September 2023.

LGM Movie Overview

  • Movie Name: Let’s Get Married
  • Directed by: Ramesh Thamilmani
  • Producers: Sakshi Singh Dhoni, Sharmila J. Rajaa, Vikas Hasija, M.V.M Velmohan
  • Starring: Harish Kalyan, Nadhiya, Ivana
  • Music by: Ramesh Thamilmani
  • Entertainment Company: Dhoni Entertainment
  • Cinematography: Viswajith Odukkathil
  • Editor: Pradeep E. Ragav
  • Release Date: July 28, 2023
  • OTT Release Date: To Be Announced
  • OTT Platform: To Be Announced

The film received mixed reviews from critics upon its theatrical release. Produced by Dhoni Entertainment, “LGM” is anticipated to continue its revenue collection once it lands on OTT platforms. While its initial box office performance was decent, it picked up steam in the following days. It made 1.5 crores on day one, followed by 2 crores on the second day, 2.5 crores on the third day, and 0.6 crores on the fourth day, bringing its total collection to 6.6 crores.

Fans of “Let’s Get Married” can soon relish the film’s delightful storyline and performances from the comfort of their homes. As the OTT release date inches closer, more details about its availability will likely be shared, so stay tuned for your date with “LGM” on your preferred OTT platform!
