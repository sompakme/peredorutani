What is the origin of the name “pizookie”?

Pizookie is an anciet Latin phrase that refers to the tastiest dessert on the planet. Its qualities are described by combining the terms “pizza” and “cookie,” which are both Italian words. In comparison to a pizza, it has a cookie-like texture and delicious taste.
