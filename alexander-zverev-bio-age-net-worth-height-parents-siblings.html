Alexander Zverev Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Parents, Siblings

Tennis player Alexander Zverev is a native of Germany. He was born in Hamburg on April 20, 1997.

One of the most skilled and promising young tennis players, Zverev is well recognized.

Zverev hails from a tennis-playing family since both of his parents and older brother are players. He took up tennis at an early age and soon established himself in junior competitions.

Alexander Zverev won the boys’ singles championship at the Australian Open in 2014 and advanced to the US Open Junior Championships final.
