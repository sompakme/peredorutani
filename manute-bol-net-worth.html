Manute Bol Net Worth

Manute Bol was a Sudanese Basketball player born on October 16, 1962 in Gogrial, South Sudan. He was one of the tallest players to ever play the game, standing at almost 7'7". Bol was a two-time NBA blocks leader, leading the Washington Bullets in 1986 and the Golden State Warriors in 1989.
