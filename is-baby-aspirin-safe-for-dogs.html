Is baby aspirin safe for dogs?

While you can use human baby aspirin as recommended by your vet, aspirin made for dogs is typically a better option. Human aspirin has a coating on it that helps to protect the human stomach from irritation. Your dog cannot digest this coating, so the medication may not provide the desired effects.

How many baby aspirin can I give my dog?

Dogs 5 to 10 pounds can have one baby aspirin pill at a time. Then, dogs 10 to 20 pounds might be able to have one and a half to two baby aspirins. But at that point, it might be easier to give them a half adult aspirin pill. Again, these aspirin doses for dogs are only a general recommendation.

Why is 81 mg aspirin not 80?

Aspirin dosing is rooted in this history. The standard adult aspirin dose was 5 gr, or 325 mg in metric, the dose still used today for analgesia. Low-dose aspirin was one quarter of the standard dose, 1.25 grains, which converted to 81 mg. This dosing regimen has persisted to modern times.
