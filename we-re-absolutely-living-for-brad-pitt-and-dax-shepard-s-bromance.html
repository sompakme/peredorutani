We're Absolutely Living For Brad Pitt and Dax Shepard's Bromance

We love a good, old-fashioned bromance, and it looks like we need to welcome two of Hollywood's newest BFFs to the party.

Brad Pitt revealed during a clip on a September 2019 episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show that he had a "little bit of a crush" on actor Dax Shepard...with Dax himself on set.

As soon as Ellen played back the recording for Dax, he had a total meltdown – as nearly anyone who's just been told Brad Pitt has a "crush" on them probably would. In fact, he told Ellen on Tuesday (January 21) that he still isn't "over it" on a return visit to her show.

"First of all, dangerous move for you to just do as a surprise while I was guest hosting because, as I've learned, there's a lot of balls in the air here. OK? You're juggling really quickly," he joked with Ellen. "And, you get hit with something like that—love of your life telling you it's not unrequited, in fact, he likes you back—that's overwhelming. But I proceeded on and we finished the show."

Shepard revealed something even cooler than the fact hat he still wasn't over Pitt's reveal, though: the two went on a "date" together!

"I went on a date with him," Shepard gushed. "I'm not kidding you. In Brad Pitt fashion, it was spectacular. We took a helicopter—you think I'm lying—we took a helicopter to a motorcycle track. We both love riding motorcycles. And just the two of us just took a jaunt up to this motorcycle track." Of course, Shepard has been married to actress Kristen Bell since 2013, so this wasn't a romantic date – but the beginning of a beautiful bromance, nevertheless.

"I felt like Pretty Woman," Shepard continued. "I felt like Julia Roberts in Pretty Woman. I was waiting for him to set that chopper down on Rodeo and let me just go wild in one of those stores with his credit card. If it had happened, it wouldn't have surprised me. It was that incredible."

He called Pitt a "warrior on two wheels" thanks to his motorcycle prowess as well, though he didn't get a chance to check out the actor's six-pack given that the pair were decked out in all leather. He's got a plan for that next time, though.

"Next date it'll hopefully be some kind of beach community where we can splash around a little bit," he said. "Brad, let's hit the beach, my friend."
