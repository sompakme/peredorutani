Mohbad Died Before He Died: ID Cabasa Claims Singers Parents Contributed to His Demise

  • ID Cabasa has claimed Mohbad's parents' separation contributed to his untimely death
  • He made the revelation on Nedu Wazobia's podcast while talking about the influence of parents on their children
  • He said if Mohbad's parents were still together, it is possible that the singer would be alive now

Veteran producer Olamide Ogunnade, better known as ID Cabasa, said the late singer Ileriolwa Aloba, aka Mohbad, might not have ended the way he did if his parents were still together.

Cabasa, who has been clamouring for justice for Mohbad, was a guest on Nedu Wazobia's FM podcast.

According to him, children learn from what they see from their parents but Mohbad's parents have not been together for a long time, so he had little or nothing to learn from them.

ID Casaba says corruption starts from the home

During the show, ID Cabasa noted that fraud starts from the home.

He said most parents take six pieces of meat and tell their children to share only one.

The producer said since children learn from what they see, they try to imitate what their parents do in other aspects of their lives.
