Are Kylie Jenner and Timothe Chalamet Dating? Internet Rife with Romance Rumors

Rumors have emerged that Timothée Chalamet and Kylie Jenner might be dating each other, and social media just can not seem to keep calm. The internet is now having a meltdown over the reports of the newest couple in town.

The rumors first began when a celebrity gossip website posted an update about the duo’s romance. The news comes just months after the two stars met each other for the first time at Jean Paul Gaultier’s Spring 2023 Haute Couture show in January.

Are Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet Dating?

Celebrity gossip website DeuxMoi, which is known for its blind items, first posted about Jenner and Chalamet’s affair on its Instagram story on April 6. “multiple sources have told me that Timmy C has a new girl…Kylie Jenner. ⚰️⚰️,” read the post. DeuxMoi then doubled down on the claims with multiple posts.

“I heard they are both going to be at Coachella,” read another anonymous tip on its Instagram page. A third post said, “I can 100% confirm it.” There are also rumors about the couple being together since the Paris Fashion Week in January. However, neither Kylie nor Timothée has commented on the rumors. The rumors could very well be false since they have not been confirmed by any credible source till now.

Jenner Broke Up with Travis Scott in January

The 25-year-old cosmetics mogul was previously in an on-and-off relationship with Travis Scott. The ex-couple shares two children together, Stormi and Aire Webster. They decided to go their separate ways in January this year due to ‘differences in their lifestyle.’ 

At the time, a source close to the development revealed, “Kylie is very focused on her kids and her business. She is not a big partier. Travis is the opposite. He likes to party. They definitely have different focuses. They always did.”

As for Chalamet, the Dune star was previously linked to Johnny Depp’s daughter Lily-Rose Depp. The couple was in a relationship for a year before breaking up in 2020. The news of Kylie’s romance with Chalamet comes at a time when her sister, Kendall, is also making headlines for her alleged relationship with Bad Bunny.

She was seen with the rapper on a horse riding date at the Hidden Hills Equestrian Centre last month. An insider recently opened up about Kendall’s equation with Bad Bunny and said, “She likes him and is having fun. He is different from guys that she dated in the past. He is very charming.”
