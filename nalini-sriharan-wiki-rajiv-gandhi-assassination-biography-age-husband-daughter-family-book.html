Nalini Sriharan Arrest (Rajiv Gandhi Assassination Case)

Indian former Prime Minister named Rajiv Gandhi was assassinated by Thenmozhi Rajaratnam on 21 May 1991 in Sriperumbudur. After the investigation, the police arrested around 24 people, and out of the 7 were the convicted persons who were directly involved in the assassination. Nalini along with her spouse Murugan was also one of them.

The court gave death-sentenced the 4 people including Nalini in May 1999 after her arrest. In 2000, Sonia Gandhi commuted Nalini’s death sentence to life imprisonment because she gave birth to a baby girl. In 2010, she went to Madras High Court to plea for her release because she served more than 20 years in jail.

Well, the court rejected her plea. As per the reports, she attempted suicide inside Vellore Special Prison for Women in 2020 but later the police confirmed that she just threatened to attempt suicide. She was granted parole a few times for the emergency by the court only. Currently, she is serving her imprisonment.
