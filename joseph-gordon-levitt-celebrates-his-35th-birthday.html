Joseph Gordon-Levitt Celebrates His 35th Birthday

Today is Joseph Gordon-Levitt's 35th birthday and one thing's certain: He's got the good looks and charm to make us swoon for days.

Gordon-Levitt started out a child star and made an impact in Hollywood with major motion pictures like 1992's A River Runs Through It and Angels in the Outfield as well as the TV show 3rd Rock from the Sun. And since 1999's 10 Things I Hate About You, the hunky Hollywood actor's career has been full speed ahead. After a brief acting break to study at Columbia University, Gordon-Levitt returned to acting in 2004. He's since appeared in 500 Days of Summer, Inception, and The Dark Knight Rises.

When the birthday boy isn't in front of the camera, he's busy working as a filmmaker. In 2004, he founded the online production company, hitRECord. Then, in 2013, he made his debut as director and screenwriter of a feature film with the release of Don Jon, a comedy film in which he also played the main role. Today, the do-it-all A-lister is gearing up for the release of his latest non-fiction film, Snowden, in which he plays Edward Snowden, a former CIA employee under fire for reportedly releasing classified information from the U.S. National Security Agency. The much-talked-about film hits theaters May 13.

With so many projects on his plate, Gordon-Levitt still manages to always arrive fresh-faced and dressed to the nines. Whether he's walking the red carpet or spending the day with wife, businesswoman Tasha McCauley, and their baby boy, Gordon-Levitt always brings his A-game in the looks department.
