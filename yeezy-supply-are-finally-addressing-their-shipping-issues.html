YEEZY Supply Are Finally Addressing Their Shipping Issues

If you've been waiting on some Yeezys or some clothes from Kanye West's website "YEEZY Supply" and don't know where your stuff is, you may get some answers soon.

The company is meeting with the Better Business Bureau today after hundreds of complaints were filed due to slow shipping times and in some cases, people just never receiving their orders. According to Hypebeast, 300 complaints were filed in October and another 189 in November. Kanye's signature clothing company is also being accused of being unresponsive to people's complaints, giving customers more questions than answers. 

Stock numbers on Yeezy's have increased over the last dew drops, which has made it a lot easier to acquire the coveted shows, which used to be near-impossible to cop.

The Static Yeezy 350 V2 will be releasing soon so YEEZY Supply will want to get their shipping issues in order before the next drop. Considering the price and popularity of the shoes, it only makes sense that people are mad about the delays.
