Anyone who’s stepped foot in an Anthropologie has most likely had the thought of living in the store. I mean, who wouldn’t? However, anyone who has stepped into the store or shopped the site knows just how expensive Anthropologie can be.

The smallest version is about three feet and costs $548 before taxes, shipping, and handling. I don’t know about you, but that seems way too pricey, right?
