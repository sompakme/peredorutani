Samir Hussein//Getty Images

Harry and Meghan are today visiting Sussex together for the first time, the county that they are Duke and Duchess of.

For the occasion – which will involve a number of engagements, including stops in Chichester, Bognor Regis and Brighton – Meghan provided all the new-season workwear inspiration we could possibly need with her elegant ensemble.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

The duchess wore a leather Hugo Boss skirt, which she teamed with a black button-up shirt by & Other Stories, a cashmere Giorgio Armani coat, Stuart Weitzman shoes, a green Gabriela Hearst bag and a Missoma signet ring.

This is the first time we have seen Meghan in a leather skirt since she became a fully-fledged member of the royal family – and her styling is enough to convince us that we desperately need a leather pencil skirt in our wardrobes this autumn.
