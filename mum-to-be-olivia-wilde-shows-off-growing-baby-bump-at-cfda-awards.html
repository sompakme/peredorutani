Mum-to-be Olivia Wilde shows off growing baby bump at CFDA Awards

    Olivia Wilde gave a masterclass in maternity style as she stepped out at the CFDA Awards in New York on Monday night. The mum-to-be wowed in a green Rosie Assoulin dress with a cut out panel, which she said allowed her unborn baby to "clearly hear Beyoncé's speech".

    The actress proudly cradled her growing baby bump as she walked the red carpet at the Hammerstein Ballroom, explaining to that she doesn't think pregnant women should attempt to hide their baby bumps.

    Olivia Wilde cradled her baby bump on the red carpet at the CFDA Awards

    "It is a gorgeous time. And there is no reason to hide it," Olivia said.

    Olivia, 32, announced that she is expecting her second child with fiancé Jason Sudeikis in April. The actress revealed the news with an adorable photo of herself and her two-year-old son Otis Alexander both holding their tummies and smiling.

    "Matching baby bumps," she wrote.

    The actress had been open about her desire to give little Otis a sibling, telling she was "desperate" to have more children.

    The actress is expecting her second child with Jason Sudeikis

    "I love my siblings so much and Jason has incredible siblings too. The more the merrier." She added that her son is also ready to be a big brother – sort of. "He loves little babies," Olivia said. "He has a Michael Jordan doll that he carries around sometimes. (He uses the doll) as either a hammer or a baby."

    In April, Olivia opened up about first meeting Jason, who popped the question in 2013. Speaking on the Howard Stern radio show, Olivia revealed that Jason used an enigmatic pick-up line when they first met backstage at a  party in 2011. "He came up to me… right up in my face and said, 'Whatever you're looking for, you don't need it', and he walked away. "He didn't even try to pick me up, he just walked away. It was so mysterious, that's why I liked it."
