Michael Jordan's Son Gets Engaged

Twitter

Congrats to the basketball pro's kid!

TMZ reports that Michael Jordan's oldest son, Jeffrey Jordan, is engaged to girlfriend Radina Aneva. And it'd appear that the 29-year-old former college basketball player, who goes by the nickname Heir Jordan, spared no expense when he chose his future bride's engagement ring. He shared a black-and-white photo of the rock on his Instagram page at the start of 2018, showing he and his fianceé opening the binder of a "marriage planning" binder.

"Turning the page," Jeffrey wrote in the caption, alongside the hashtag, "#happynewyear2018."

But Jeffrey didn't pop the question at the end of the holiday season. According to People, Jeffrey asked Aneva to marry him back in November 2017 while the two were vacationing in Hawaii.

For her part, Aneva, who reportedly works for 20th Century Fox, seems just as excited as Jeffrey to get started on building a life together. While she's since made her Instagram account private, People magazine noted that she shared the big news with her followers in late November, writing, "I said YES!"

She later shared a photo with the caption, "Thankful for all the growth, life lessons, success and love I experienced this year. Looking forward to planning the future and starting a family with my best friend."

Congratulations to the happy couple!
