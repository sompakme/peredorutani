Saint West Rocks Cornrows - Kim Kardashian's Son Saint West Has Cornrows

Leave it to Kim Kardashian and Kanye West's one-year-old son to turn heads with his new hairstyle. Saint West was spotted rocking itty-bitty cornrows yesterday, and he looked too cute for words.

Splash News

The tot was apparently filming a scene for Keeping Up with the Kardashians with his parents and four-year-old sister, North West, at a skating rink in Los Angeles. When photos of his new 'do hit the internet, fans couldn't stop gushing.

Although Saint's hair was the main event, his outfit was also on point. He wore a Harley Davidson baseball tee and sweats with Vans sneakers (with a candy bar in hand, of course). The youngest of the Kardashian-West brood is definitely taking style notes from mom, dad and his big sis.
