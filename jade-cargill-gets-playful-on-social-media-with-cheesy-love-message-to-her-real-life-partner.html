Jade Cargill Gets Playful on Social Media with Cheesy Love Message to Her Real-Life Partner

WWE sensation Jade Cargill just unleashed some real-life love vibes on social media, and it’s got us all swooning!

Now, let’s talk about Jade’s wrestling journey. She’s been turning heads on RAW, NXT, and SmackDown, and fans have been scratching their heads, wondering which brand she’ll ultimately call home. But before she stepped into the WWE ring, Jade had an epic run over at AEW, where she held the TBS Championship for a record-breaking 508 days. Yeah, she’s kind of a big deal.
