FAQs

Does Spanx offer free shipping?

Spanx offers free standard shipping on orders placed on the website. If you want your order a little sooner rather than later, you can always opt for one of the expedited shipping options. If you choose one of them, however, you will need to pay a shipping fee.

Are there Spanx sales?

Spanx sales happen periodically throughout the year, often falling around holidays. Signing up for the newsletter or text alerts is a good way to stay informed of upcoming sales. If you’re in a hurry to get your Spanx, however, you can always check out the sales section on the website.  You can also check our website for a Spanx coupon code.

Does Spanx have an email newsletter welcome offer?

Spanx has an email-based newsletter that you can sign up for to get 10% off your purchase. There is also a text-based program that will keep you up to date on upcoming Spanx promo codes, new products, and exclusive offers. Both are free to join and you can opt out at any time. To sign up, enter your email or phone number into the appropriate form at the bottom of the Spanx homepage.

Where can I find a Spanx influencer code?

Unfortunately, there isn't a Spanx influencer code available at this time. If this changes, we will update this page with all the necessary information. In the meantime, you can save on your future orders with a Spanx coupon code.

What is the Spanx return policy?

You can return your Spanx products within 30 days of your delivery date. Your product should be unworn and undamaged. If you are returning swimwear, the protective liner should still be attached. Final sale items cannot be returned. There is a $7.95 return shipping fee that will be deducted from your total refund amount. Items that are exchanged will not incur a refund fee.
