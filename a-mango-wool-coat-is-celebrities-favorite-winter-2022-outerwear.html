A Mango Wool Coat Is Celebrities' Favorite Winter 2022 Outerwear

Our days of piling on heavy jackets and balaclavas are numbered, thankfully. But before switching gears from faux shearling and thermal layers to sundresses and crochet everything, celebrities are making the case for shopping one more everyday coat.

Alexa Chung, Gigi Hadid, Sienna Miller, and Katie Holmes have all topped their late-winter outfits with Mango's double-breasted wool coat this month. Everything from the cut (slightly oversized, yet tailored) to the color (a wintry gray-cream blend) to the price (affordable, at $300), says, "Why not buy?" The coat is also part of Mango's Committed collection, meaning it's partially made with eco-friendly materials.

Celebrities' current go-to Mango coat is a piece you'll never second guess. Layered with a plaid blazer and split-hem leggings like Gigi Hadid, the coat is a finishing touch for weekday errands or weekend brunch. For the most laid-back of looks, go the Sienna Miller route with denim and Uggs, or layer with monochromatic knits like Katie Holmes.

Mango Double-Breasted Coat

Credit: Mango

Celebrities (or often, their stylists) shop Mango's outerwear section almost every winter. Last year, Gigi Hadid was seen wearing a plaid wool coat by Mango while walking in New York City with her daughter, Khai. This January, Katie Holmes wore a forest green version to shop in Soho. Both those Mango coats had the same clean tailoring as the cream version taking over, and both are currently on sale.

However you decide to wear this year's celebrity-approved coat, you'll want to add to cart now. According to BAZAAR UK, there's a waiting list for the coat in Europe, and some sizes are already sold out in the U.S. Celebrities may move on to another Mango style next winter, but this double-breasted coat is a classic you'll wear every season.
