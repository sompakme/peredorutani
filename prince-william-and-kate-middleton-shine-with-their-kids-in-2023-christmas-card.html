Prince William and Kate Middleton Shine with Their Kids in 2023 Christmas Card

The Prince and Princess of Wales have released their 2023 Christmas card, featuring a black and white portrait of the family.

The snap, captured by Bazaar contributing photographer Josh Shinner earlier this year on the Windsor grounds, depicts a smiling William and Kate alongside their three children: Prince George, 10, Princess Charlotte, eight, and Prince Louis, five. The family posed for a classic portrait in which Princess Charlotte sits in a leather chair surrounded by her brothers and parents. The royal family all wore smart white shirts and black trainers, with the princesses in jeans and the princes in black trousers.
