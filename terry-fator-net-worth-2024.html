Terry Fator’s Early Life

Terry Fator was born on June 10, 1965, in Dallas, Texas. He discovered his love for ventriloquism at the young age of ten. Beginning of Terry Fator’s Career

Fator started his career performing at county fairs and theaters. His big break came in 2007 when he won the second season of America’s Got Talent. This victory catapulted him into the limelight and opened doors to numerous opportunities.
