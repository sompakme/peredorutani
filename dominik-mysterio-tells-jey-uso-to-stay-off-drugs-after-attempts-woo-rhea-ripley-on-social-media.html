Dominik Mysterio Tells Jey Uso to Stay Off Drugs After Attempts Woo Rhea Ripley on Social Media

The ongoing storyline involving Dominik Mysterio, Jey Uso, and Rhea Ripley has been capturing the attention of WWE fans, and it seems the drama spilled over onto social media recently. Jey Uso had been posting pictures with Rhea Ripley, fueling rumors and speculation about their on-screen romance.

In response to Jey Uso’s posts, Dominik Mysterio decided to join in on the fun with a playful jab. He posted a humorous comment on one of Uso’s pictures, encouraging kids to follow their dreams while adding a comical twist with a reference to staying off drugs.

“I will always encourage kids to follow their dreams…

Brutal…..stay off drugs kids”

As the drama unfolds, fans can look forward to seeing how this storyline progresses and whether it will lead to further on-screen conflict between these WWE Superstars. The interaction on social media just adds an extra entertaining layer to the ongoing storyline between Dominik Mysterio and Jey Uso, which is expected to culminate in a showdown eventually on Monday Night RAW.
