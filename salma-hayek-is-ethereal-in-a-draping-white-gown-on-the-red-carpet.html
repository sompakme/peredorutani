Salma Hayek Is Ethereal in a Draping White Gown on the Red Carpet

Salma Hayek’s timeless red-carpet style has returned with another ethereal look.

The Eternals star made her way to the Toronto International Film Festival yesterday, where she attended the premiere of El Sabor de la Navidad at the Royal Alexandra Theatre. For the flashy occasion, Hayek opted to wear a gorgeous ivory dress featuring a form-fitting silhouette, a sleeveless bodice, draping fabric across the waist, and a long train attached at the back. To accessorize, she added a rectangular mint green clutch with matching icy-blue and white sequins and gold hardware.

As for her glam, the actor went bold with dark purple eye shadow, a bright red lip, and a glossy baby pink manicure. She straightened her layered hair and wore it down across her shoulders.

Isaiah Trickey//Getty Images

Earlier this week, Hayek made another splashy red-carpet appearance at the Kering Foundation’s second annual Caring for Women dinner in New York City, where she and husband François-Henri Pinault (chairman and CEO of Kering) were among the event’s co-chairs, in attendance with Zoë Kravitz, Tracee Ellis Ross, Kim Kardashian, Nicole Kidman, Christy Turlington Burns, Malala Yousafzai, and many other stars.

For the event, Hayek looked incredible in a crimson red halter-neck dress completely covered in glittering sequins. The dress included thin spaghetti straps and a mini V-neckline. She accessorized with a long rectangular clutch made of black nylon, dazzling diamond drop earrings, and a pair of strappy black sandals. Meanwhile, Pinault wore a classic black tux with a crisp white button-up and a matching black bow tie.
