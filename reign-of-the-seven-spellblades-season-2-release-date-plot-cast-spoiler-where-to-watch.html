Reign of the Seven Spellblades Season 2 Release Date, Plot, Cast, Spoiler &amp; Where to Watch

Reign of the Seven Spellblades Season 2: Reign of the Seven Spellblades is a fantasy anime series that premiered in July 2023 and concluded in October 2023 with 15 episodes. The series is based on a light novel series of the same name written by Bokuto Uno and illustrated by Ruria Miyuki. The anime adaptation was produced by J.C.Staff and directed by Masato Matsune, with Shōgo Yasukawa as the scriptwriter and Kujira Yumemi as the music composer.

The story follows Oliver Horn, a young mage who enrolls in the prestigious Kimberly Magic Academy, where he forms a bond with a katana-wielding girl named Nanao Hibiya. However, Oliver has a hidden motive for attending the academy: he seeks revenge against the seven members of the faculty who were involved in the death of his mother, Chloe Halford, a legendary spellblade. As Oliver and Nanao face the dangers of the academy’s labyrinths, monsters, and secrets, they also discover the truth behind the seven spellblades that rule the world.

The anime series received positive reviews from critics and fans alike, who praised its stunning visuals, action-packed scenes, intriguing plot, and likable characters. The series also has a rating of 6.74 on MyAnimeList, based on over 30,000 votes. The series was simulcasted by Crunchyroll, which also licensed the series for streaming in North America, Europe, Australia, and other regions.

Based on these factors, we can conclude that Reign of the Seven Spellblades has a high chance of getting a second season, as it has enough source material, popularity, and profitability to warrant a continuation. However, the final decision ultimately depends on the production committee, which consists of various stakeholders such as the publisher, the studio, the broadcaster, and the licensor. Plot

The plot of the second season of Reign of the Seven Spellblades will most likely follow the events of the fifth to eighth volumes of the light novel series, which are titled “The Sword of the King”, “The Sword of the Hero”, “The Sword of the Demon”, and “The Sword of the God”. These volumes will continue the story of Oliver and Nanao as they face new challenges and enemies at Kimberly Magic Academy, as well as reveal more secrets and mysteries about the seven spellblades and their origins.

Some of the major plot points that we can expect to see in the Reign of the Seven Spellblades Season 2:

  • Oliver and Nanao will participate in the Sword Festival, a tournament that pits the best swordsmen and swordswomen of the academy against each other. They will face off against other talented students, such as Carlos Whitrow, Ophelia Salvadori, and Vera Miligan, who are also the wielders of the other spellblades. They will also encounter a mysterious masked swordsman, who seems to have a connection to Oliver’s past.
  • Oliver and Nanao will learn more about the history and nature of the seven spellblades, which are actually fragments of the original spellblade, Excalibur, that was created by the ancient civilization of Atlantis. They will also discover the true identity and motive of the seventh spellblade, the Sword of the God, which is the most powerful and dangerous of all.
  • Oliver and Nanao will face the wrath of the seven faculty members, who are also the former wielders of the spellblades. They will learn the truth behind the death of Chloe Halford, and the reason why the faculty members betrayed her and tried to kill her son. They will also have to deal with the consequences of their actions, as they are branded as traitors and enemies of the academy and the world.
