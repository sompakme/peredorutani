San Jose Mourns Lillian Hanan

In a heart-wrenching incident that has left the San Jose community in deep sorrow, 1-year-old Lillian Hanan tragically lost her life at a local daycare. The news has spread like wildfire, sparking questions and immense grief among those who have come to know about this devastating incident.

A Tragic Day Unfolds

The Santa Clara County coroner’s office officially identified the young girl as Lillian Hanan from San Jose, alongside Payton Cobb of Hollister. The incident occurred around 9 a.m. in the 1000 block of Fleetwood, where a distress call brought emergency assistance after several children fell into a pool, as reported by the police. Sadly, two toddlers did not survive, and another child remains in critical condition, though not life-threatening, as per the police statement.
