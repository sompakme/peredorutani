While speaking with the press, the style mogul was asked what her favourite body part to show off is, a question the reporter said she asked Rihanna a decade ago:

Rihanna then paused and took a step back before replying:

"Oh yeah girl it changed. I guarantee you it changed. I had a baby. Let's be real. My booty- now it's my booty because I got one."

The reporter then reminded her that when she answered that question ten years ago, she still said the same answer, to which the Lift Me Up hitmaker laughed off before making a spin.

"Well the answer has not changed," she said.
