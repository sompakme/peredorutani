Verydarkman Remanded After Court Hearing Following Charges Filed By Police: &quot;Make Dem Leave Am&quot;

  • Verydarkman's friend, Tunde Ednut, revealed that the social media activist had been remanded after a court hearing in Abuja
  • According to the information shared by one of his associates, Kokopee, some charges were leveled against him by the Nigerian police
  • Kokopee noted that the activist will be remanded for one week till the next court hearing
  • In a chat with Opeyemi Famakin, Nigerian food critic, he gave his perspective on VDM's remand

Martins Otse, better known as Verydarkman (VDM), was charged with some accusations in an Abuja court on Wednesday, May 22, 2024.

In an Instagram video, one of his associates, Kokopee, narrated that VDM would be imprisoned for a week following his court hearing.

VDM will come out stronger - Kokopee

In the video that has gone viral, Kokopee did not give a specific reason VDM was charged to court.
