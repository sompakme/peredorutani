Lady Amelia Windsor is our inspiration for summer wedding-guest dressing

Lady Amelia Windsor may be one of the youngest members of the royal family, but she was definitely among the best dressed at the wedding of Lady Gabriella Windsor and Thomas Kingston on Saturday (18 May).

The 23-year-old turned heads in a bubblegum-pink midi dress printed all over with daisies, by Gül Hürgel. She teamed it with a white mini bag, espadrille wedges, earrings by Halcyon Days and a Philip Treacy fascinator.

The royals tend to keep it demure for weddings (no shoulders on show, hemlines below the knee etc), so we love how Windsor managed to keep her look playful while still looking royal wedding-appropriate.
