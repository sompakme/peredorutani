The Stylish Sneakers I Can Walk in All Day Are Back in Stock

Some people are all about Lululemon, while others appreciate the comfort of Athleta. As for me? I’m an Alo Yoga girl, through and through. From its supermodel-approved Airlift leggings and perfectly pleated skirts to its viral teddy trench coat and the killer varsity jacket I’m wearing on repeat this fall, the brand manages to nail streetwear style in a way that other athleisure brands can’t quite cut it. 

Last spring Alo Yoga also debuted its first-ever sneakers, the Alo x 01 Classic, which have sold out three times since. Luckily, the unisex kicks have recently been restocked and are now available in a new color combo, too. I managed to snag myself a pair of the OG style and totally get the hype. 

Backstory: The moment the street-meets-recovery shoes dropped into my inbox, the obsession started based solely (no pun intended) on aesthetics. If the Nike Air Force 1 had a somewhat edgier cousin, it would be the 01 Classic. The original white-on-white style looks and feels like leather and features a light brown gummy sole, and the new white and black option is equally stylish. The brand recommends sizing down as the sneakers run big. I usually wear a 9.5 so I ordered a 9 and found that they fit perfectly. 

The first thing I noticed when I slipped the shoes on my feet was the supportive fit — they really hug my feet and ankles, which makes them more than just street sneakers, but ones that I could wear during a workout, too. They feature amazing cushioning for this very reason, too. The Alo Yoga sneakers are made with a “high-rebound recovery-foam insole” to support balance and pressure points (like the knees, feet, hips, joints, and back) for “all-day recovery.” Jumping up and down in them is actually quite fun, as you can feel a little bounce. In addition to the cushioning, the recovery foam sole offers a little height (about 2 inches) that has a slimming effect on your legs, similar to a platform heel. 

Now, let's talk about how to style the 01 Classic. The sneakers are a great on-trend option to wear with everything from tennis skirts and maxi dresses to leggings and jeans. Anything you would wear a pair of white Nikes with, it will tackle with a little more edge. In terms of activities, I’ve sported my pair on the tennis court and in the gym for strength and weight training. While I don’t recommend the shoes for running, as they’re on the heavier side and don't have a curved toe, I have spent a day wearing mine around the city and can report that they make for great walking shoes. 

Just as obsessed as I am? I recommend ordering your pair of Alo x 01 Classic sneakers now — some color and size combinations are already sold out.
