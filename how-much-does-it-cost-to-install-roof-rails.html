Roof rails in automobiles serve what purpose?

Roof rails are used to transport large amounts of cargo or sporting equipment overhead. These rails can be attached to special roof mounts that can be attached to the cargo or sporting equipment. They are a modern version of the old ‘luggage carrier,’ except that they are intended to serve as mounting points rather than as carriers of luggage or other items.
