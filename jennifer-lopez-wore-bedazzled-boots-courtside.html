Jennifer Lopez Wore Bedazzled Boots Courtside

Last night's game between the Los Angeles Lakers and Golden State Warriors was a close one, but ultimately, the Warriors walked away with the win. And while there's a myriad of reasons as to why, we can't help but wonder if Jennifer Lopez's courtside shoes had anything to do with it. Yes, you read that right — J.Lo's shoes might've influenced the outcome of a basketball game. 

For the occasion, Lopez wore a pair of silvery-blue bedazzled boots that were so sparkly, you couldn't help but look down. Diverting everyone's attention away from the game (perhaps even the players), J.Lo's boots weren't just rhinestone-studded but also featured a controversial wedge heel. She teamed her eye-catching footwear with high-waisted wide-leg jeans, a cropped white T-shirt, and even more glitter in the form of a pink-and-beige sequined tweed blazer. 

A few J.Lo signatures (gold hoops, aviator sunglasses, a fistful of diamond rings) finished off her courstside outfit, while a sleek blowout, a bronzy contour, and nude lips completed her glam. 

Lopez attended the game with her husband, Ben Affleck — who dressed casually in a plaid flannel shirt, a navy blue jacket, matching trousers, and a baseball cap — as well as her 12-year-old stepson, Samuel.

J.Lo's latest family outing comes just a couple months before she embarks on her upcoming This Is Me Now... Tour, which kicks off this summer in Orlando on June 26. She will perform in other major U.S. cities, including Los Angeles, Dallas, and Miami, before concluding the tour at Madison Square Garden in New York City in August.
